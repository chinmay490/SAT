ABOUT US

Founded in 1967 by Late Mr. Sharda Prasad Mishra, Sharda Auto Traders has been a trusted name in the supply of industrial hydraulic products, building a reputation that spans generations. In 1995, Mr. Manish Mishra joined the family business, marking a strategic expansion into the pneumatics and tractor seal markets.

Over the years, our product portfolio has grown significantly, and we have built long-standing partnerships with industry leaders such as Freudenberg NOK, SPAC Pneumatics, JK Fenner Industrial Division, and JK Pioneer Tractor Division. As a leading authorised distributor of hydraulic and pneumatic components in the Vidarbha region, we proudly serve prestigious industrial clients including UltraTech Cement Ltd, Western Coalfields Ltd, Prithvi II Missile Regiment, Mercedes-Benz India Pvt Ltd, Haldiram, and many more.

Our commitment to delivering high-quality products, technical support, and exceptional customer service has earned us the trust and loyalty of our clients. With a legacy of success spanning over 50 years, Sharda Auto Traders continues to lead in industrial solutions and remains dedicated to future growth and expanding customer value.
