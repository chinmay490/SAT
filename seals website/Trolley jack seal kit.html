Trolley Jack and Bottle Jack Seal Kit

Trolley Jack and Bottle Jack Seal kit are set of replacement seals designed specifically for Trolley Jacks and Bottle Jacks. These kits typically include various types of seals, such as U-seals, Wiper Seals, O-Rings and different types of washer seals necessary for repairing and maintaining hydraulic Trolley and Bottle Jacks. By replacing worn or damaged seals with components from the kit, users can restore the hydraulic system's performance, preventing leaks and ensuring smooth operation.

Materials:-

  • PU (Polyurethane)
  • Rubber
