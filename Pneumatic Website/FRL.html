Filter Regulator Lubricator (FRL) units

The Filter Regulator and Lubricator (FRL) system maintains pneumatic systems by managing the three critical factors affecting air quality through contaminant filtration and pressure stabilization and lubricant distribution. To achieve this, FRL units are engineered with threaded ports for in-line installation within pneumatic systems, often constructed from durable materials like metal or high-strength polymers. Their modular design allows for easy customization and maintenance because every component operates independently and are replaceable or serviceable as needed. The air filter functions by eliminating contaminants while the pressure regulator controls airflow pressure and the lubricator adds a fine mist of oil to the airflow cycle that increases tool capacity along with tool equipment longevity, thereby enhancing the performance and extending the lifespan of pneumatic tools and equipment.
